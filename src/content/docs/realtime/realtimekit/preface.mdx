To see and interact with other participants, all selected participants must join the same meeting. Cross meeting participants cannot interact with each other, except in case of breakout rooms.

To achieve this, you will need to follow the steps below:

1. [Create a meeting](/realtime/realtimekit/meeting-creation/)
2. [Create a preset](/realtime/realtimekit/preset-creation/)
3. [Create a participant](/realtime/realtimekit/participant-creation/)
4. [Initialize the SDK](/realtime/realtimekit/initialize-sdk/)
5. [Join the meeting](/realtime/realtimekit/initialize-sdk/#join-the-meeting)
6. [Create 2nd participant](/realtime/realtimekit/participant-creation/)
7. [Initialize the SDK for 2nd participant in a different tab/device](/realtime/realtimekit/initialize-sdk/)
8. [Join the meeting as 2nd participant in a different tab/device](/realtime/realtimekit/initialize-sdk/#join-the-meeting)
9. Render their videos, and play their audio so that they can see an hear each other.

Once you have completed the above steps, you will see 2 participants in the meeting in each tab.

These participants, now, can interact with each other in real-time.

:::note
While testing in 2 tabs/devices, you might hear your own audio. This is because the audio is played back to you in the same tab/device where it was captured, creating a feedback loop.
In real world, each participant will have their own device and browser, and they will not hear their own audio.
:::

In upcoming guides, we will cover all of the above steps in detail and explain how to integrate the SDK in your app or website.
